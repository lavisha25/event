About

Welcome to the Developer Student Club at Chitkara University, Rajpura, Punjab. We are a community group for students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term. By joining is, students build their professional and personal network, get access to valuable resources, and wok logons to build solutions for local problems in a peer-to-peer learning environment.

What to do:

  • Hands-on coding workshops(Beginner and Experienced)
  • Build side projects
  • Compete in the Solution Challenge
  • Networking events Cloud Study Jams
  • DG/DE expert talks
